Who We Are

A Foundation Built on
Compassion & Action

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit committed to creating lasting social impact. With our base in Chhattisgarh, we operate across India — addressing critical societal challenges through a network of dedicated volunteers and professionals.

We believe every individual deserves to live with dignity and respect. Our mission is to serve communities compassionately and our vision is to create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow.

Our Initiatives

Our 6 Flagship Projects

Each project is designed to address a specific societal challenge, with measurable outcomes and real community impact.

🍱

Project SEVA

Providing nutritious meals, clothing, and essential supplies to the homeless and underprivileged. No one should sleep on an empty stomach — SEVA ensures they don't.

🌟 50,000+ meals served
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through digital literacy training, life skills workshops, and school education support — giving every child a chance.

📖 Digital literacy for hundreds
👩‍💼

Project Udaan

Empowering women through vocational skill development, financial literacy, and mentorship programs — enabling economic independence and self-reliance.

💪 Women-led livelihood creation
🌿

Project Prakriti

Driving environmental conservation through tree-plantation drives, waste management awareness, and sustainable development initiatives that protect our planet for future generations.

🌱 Green drives across regions
🐾

Project Jeev

Dedicated to animal welfare — feeding stray animals, providing medical care, and raising awareness about ethical and compassionate treatment of all living beings.

🐕 Regular animal feeding drives
💼

Project Vikas

Enhancing the employability of youth through structured internship programs, skill training, and career-oriented workshops — preparing a generation of changemakers.

🚀 30,000+ interns trained
Social Impact

Why Our Work
Matters

In a world where millions still lack access to basic nutrition, quality education, and economic opportunity, InAmigos acts as a bridge — connecting compassion to action across Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh.

With full transparency and government oversight, every rupee donated goes directly into changing lives. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification ensures the highest operational standards.
